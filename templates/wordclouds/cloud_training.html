The goal of this task is to find synonyms and related words for each word in a sentence.

Add synonyms for a word by typing them into the "add word" box and pressing 'enter'.

Then, drag more related words from the Word Bank on the left. Only include words that have the same meaning in the context of the sentence.

Notice that each word has two bins, 'concrete' and 'abstract'. Put direct synonyms and 'specific' words into the concrete bin, and more generic words into the 'abstract' bin. For example, 'fry' is a specific synonym of cook, while 'make' is an abstract (more generic) synonym of cook.

We've already done 'dog' and 'run' as examples. Complete the 'cook' column by dragging in words from the Word Bank and adding some of your own.
